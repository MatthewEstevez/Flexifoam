Platform Features

⚡

Rapid Subtractive Cutting

Unlike additive 3D printing, subtractive foam cutting eliminates layer-by-layer build time. Complex parts emerge in minutes, ready to test, iterate, and improve the same day.

🔄

Multi-Axis Motion

XYZ linear axes paired with a rotary axis enable geometries impossible with conventional foam tools. Undercuts, compound curves, and complex profiles are within reach.

🔧

Tool-Agnostic Platform

Engineered around a flexible toolhead interface supporting hot wire, blade, and future cutting methods, all without major hardware changes between setups.

🔒

Fully Enclosed & Safe

A rigid enclosure keeps operations contained and repeatable. Designed for lab, studio, and small production environments where safety compliance is non-negotiable.

💻

Software-Driven Control

Every cut is defined in software, from toolpath generation to motion control. No manual calibration, no guesswork. Just import, configure, and run.

📐

Large Build Volume

Maintain the scale advantages of foam without sacrificing precision. FlexiFoam supports large workpieces that would be impractical or cost-prohibitive in other prototyping methods.

02

How It Works

01

Design Your Part

Create your design in any CAD software. Export it to a supported format. FlexiFoam works with common toolpath standards.

02

Generate Toolpaths

The software converts your design into precise motion sequences. It optimizes the cutting strategy for the tool and foam material.

03

Load & Run

Secure the foam, choose your cutting tool, and start. The enclosed platform does the rest safely and consistently.

04

Iterate Fast

Parts are ready in minutes. Test, improve your design, and create another version. You can repeat this process many times in one session.
